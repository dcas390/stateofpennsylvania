Popular Cities in Pennsylvania

  1. The City of Harrisburg

    Harrisburg is the capital of Pennsylvania and is located along the Susquehanna River. It serves as the political hub of the state and has a rich history that dates back to the 18th century.

  2. The City of Lancaster

    Lancaster is known for its Amish community and rich agricultural heritage, often referred to as the heart of Pennsylvania Dutch Country.

  3. The City of Philadelphia

    Known as the "City of Brotherly Love," Philadelphia is the largest city in Pennsylvania and is rich in American history.
