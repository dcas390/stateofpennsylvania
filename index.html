Discover the beauty Pennsylvania has to offer!

Pennsylvania is called "The Keystone State", this is because PA was the middle colony out of the 13 original colonies. PA has held a key position in a few things like economic, social, and political development of the US.

One of th cities in PA, Philadelphia, was actually the site of the first and second Continental Congresses. It happens to be where the Declaration of Independence was created. After the American revolution war, this state became one of the first five to ratify the U.S. Constitution.
