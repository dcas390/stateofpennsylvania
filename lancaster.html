Below you will find some additional facts of this beautiful city.

Population of Lancaster

Lancaster has an estimated population about 58,039 people as of the census of 2023.

(Source: Lancaster Wikipedia page.)

The Year Lancaster was incorporated

Lancaster was incorporated initially as a borough in the year of 1742 then in 1818 it was incorporated as a City.

(Source: Lancaster Wikipedia page.)

The Region Lancaster is located

The city of Lancaster is located in South Central Pennsylvania, it runs along the Susquehanna River and is "approximately 70 miles west of Philadelphia." (Source: About-Lancaster-County.)

Average Income Level

The average household income for Lancaster is slightly lower than the state average. In 2023 the state of Pennsylvania had an average household income of approximately $79,820. While Lancaster's average is closer to $61,014.
