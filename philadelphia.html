Welcome to Philadelphia, Pennsylvania!

Below you will find some information of this city.

Population of Philadelphia

Philadelphia is a large city, actually it is one of the largest cities in Pennsylvania. It is estimated to have about 1,550,542 people as of the most recent census.

(Source: Census of Philadelphia.)

The Year Philadelphia was incorporated

Philadelphia became incorporated as a city in the year 1682 by William Penn and grew into a colonial city.

(Source: Philadelphia Wikipedia page)

The Region Philadelphia is located

The region that Philadelphia is located in is Southeastern Pennsylvania. "Southeastern Pennsylvania Transportation Authority (SEPTA) is the body that is in charge of public transportation in Philadelphia and its surrounding suburbs. It has bus, subway, trolley and commuter rail services".

(Source: Wiki-Philadelphia Region.)

Average Income Level

The average household income for Philadelphia is a bit lower than the whole state average. In 2023 the state of Pennsylvania had an average household income of approximately $79,820. While Philadelphia's average is closer to $60,302.
